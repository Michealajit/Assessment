1.Introduction

Rethink your company’s cyber security

In August 2019, the ISO announced a new certification ISO/IEC 27701:2019 also known as the Privacy Information Management System or PIMS. Outsourcing is on the rise despite increasing cyber security & data beaches. In today’s challenging world of Blockchain, AI, IoT and Cloud, you need to be a step ahead of your competitors. Getting certified in ISO/IEC 27001 may not be enough, hence, ISO/IEC 27701 was published to provide guidance for establishing, implementing, maintaining & continually improving PIMS in the form of an extension to ISO/IEC 27001 and 27002 for privacy management.

2. Program Highlight

The ISO/IEC 27701

The ISO/IEC 27701 Lead Auditor training course will enable you independently perform a Privacy Information Management System (PIMS) audit by developing the necessary skills and knowledge

You will learn practical knowledge on ISO/IEC 27701 by applying widely recognized audit principles, procedures & techniques.

Why should you attend?

The Lead Auditor Training for ISO/IEC 27701 enables the participant to gain the necessary expertise & knowledge which can help them conduct audits as per the compliance requirements of ISO 19011 and the certification process which is in accordance with ISO/IEC 17021-1. Participants will gain in depth knowledge on privacy in terms of PII (personally identifiable information), privacy information management system (PIMS) as well as on audit planning.

The course includes practical exercises with which the participant will become competent to independently manage the audit plan / program, audit team, communication for audit & conflict management.

Who should get this training?

  • Anyone who wants to perform & lead ISO/IEC 27701 (PIMS) audits
  • Anyone who wants to get into ISO/IEC 27001 consultancy/management
  • Anyone who needs to maintain PIMS as per its requirements in their organization

What will you gain?

After successful completion of this course, the participant can give the exam for obtaining the “Certificate for ISO/IEC 27701 Lead Auditor Training”. This certificate shows that the participant has the competency to audit organizations as per the requirements for ISO/IEC 27701 – Privacy Information Management System.
